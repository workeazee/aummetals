Trading

We are a trading company involved in importing & trading of Heat Resistant Scraps, Stainless Steel Scraps, Duplex Steel Scraps, Tungsten, Tungsten Carbide and Cobalt alloys, Manganese Steel Scraps & other high nickel / chromium / moly content bearing scraps required by foundries manufacturing Heat Resistant Steel, Stainless steel, Manganese Steel, High Chrome Steel. These scrap metals are also required by Stainless Steel plants. We also supply various pure metal scraps & their alloys such as titanium scraps, tungsten scraps, moly scraps, etc.

We regularly source our materials through vendors, imports & through auctions / tenders from throughout India from the participating plants such as Fertilizer plants, Steel plants, Cement plants, Chemical plants, Textile plants, etc.

We have a team of highly efficient and skilled professionals which have all the necessary and updated knowledge of metal scraps as well as market trends and demands. This enables them to provide service as per the specific demands made by our esteemed clients. Due to this, we are able to gain a huge client base across the country.

Industries that we cater to are Foundries, Casting & Forging units, Steel Plants, Melting Furnaces & other Heavy Industries in Engineering, Railway, Defense, Electrical, etc.
